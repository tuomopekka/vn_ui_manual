# CLAUDE.md

## Design Context

This is the Valtioneuvosto (Finnish Government) and 12-ministry shared design system. `design.md` is the source-of-truth spec; `index.html` is the documentation/style-guide site that renders it. Full detail in `PRODUCT.md`.

- **Register:** product (reference tool, not marketing)
- **Primary users:** internal designers/devs, plus external vendors/agencies with zero prior context who must self-serve a correct implementation
- **Brand personality:** official, calm, expert-confident — no unnecessary visual effects
- **Anti-references:** generic consumer SaaS/startup aesthetics; cluttered or inconsistent EU/Nordic gov sites
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 A/AA is mandatory (digipalvelulaki), not optional — see `design.md` §2 for binding specifics (focus indicators, contrast, touch targets, reduced motion, landmarks, `sr-only` usage)

See `PRODUCT.md` for the full users/purpose/principles writeup before making design decisions.
